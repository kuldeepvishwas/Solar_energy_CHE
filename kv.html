How Solar Panels Work

Solar panels work by converting sunlight into electricity through the use of photovoltaic cells. When sunlight hits a solar panel, it excites the electrons in the cells, creating a flow of electricity. This electricity can be used to power homes and businesses, or stored in batteries for later use.
